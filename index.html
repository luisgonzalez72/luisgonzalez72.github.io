Why can art be dangerous?

Art is dangerous, not because it is beautiful, but because it pushes us to think and look into an immeasurable abyss. That is why the metaphors of art extend towards infinity and one shapes them with their own interpretation and experience..

ARTURO TORRES

Arturo Torres is a Dallas-based artist. He has notably collaborated with Shea Serrano on three New York Times best-selling books in which Torres illustrated Serrano's essays.

NAIF BY TESSA IA

The EP tells us about love, friendship, but also about family, loyalty and issues with which we relate every day, how life closes the doors for us but in the end we find the way out.

FILIP CUSTIC

Filip Custic is not just an artist. He is an illusionist, a juggler who has fun with art, gravity and the laws of attraction. His mind, generous and open to the world, explores and plays with pataphysics, the science that studies irregularities, rarities and exceptions.
